Why Adero

Designed for classrooms, built for the real world.

Our sessions move fast between real talk and real skills — universal design, assistive technology, and the confidence to build for people who don't look, move, or learn like you do. Every workshop leaves students with a framework they can use long after we've left the room.

Hands-on, not hypothetical

Students test real interfaces and real barriers, then redesign them — accessibility as a design practice, not a lecture.

Built for every school

Curriculum flexes for private and public school schedules, grade bands, and class sizes — half-day, full-day, or multi-week.

Led with candor

Adero speaks from lived experience about confidence, resilience, and building a career on your own terms — students listen because it's honest.
